My Paint Scheme for Vior'la Sept

Prime with White spray can

Battle Ready

Paints in order

  1. Base coat the cloth with Eshin Grey
  2. Base coat the hoofs with Fenrisian Grey
  3. Base coat the parts of the gun you want to be red with Fire Dragon Bright
  4. Paint the other parts of the gun with Contrast Black Templar
  5. Wash the cloth with Contrast Black Templar (thin slightly with contrast medium)
  6. Wash the red parts of the gun with Contrast Blood Angels Red
  7. Tidy up the armour and any other small parts with a white paint
  8. Wash the hoofs with contrast Space Wolves Grey
  9. Wash any white with Contrast Apothecary White
  10. Tidy up any panels that are too grey with white paint.
  11. Paint the circular icons with Retributor Armour
  12. Wash the circular icons with Contrast Guilliman Flesh
  13. Paint the hoof nails with Mournfang Brown
  14. Pant the eye lenses with Contrast Blood Angels Red
  15. Highlight cloth with Eshin, followed by Dawnstone
  16. Paint any markings with Mephiston Red
