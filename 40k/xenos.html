Xenos Factions in Warhammer 40,000

"Xenos" is the term used by the Imperium of Man to refer to all alien species. These species are often seen as threats to humanity and are typically enemies of the Imperium. Here are some of the major xenos factions:

Eldar (Aeldari)

  • An ancient and highly advanced race with powerful psychic abilities.
  • Split into various sub-factions:
    • Craftworld Eldar: Live on massive starships called Craftworlds.
    • Dark Eldar (Drukhari): Reside in the dark city of Commorragh, known for their piratical and sadistic behavior.
    • Harlequins: A faction dedicated to preserving Eldar culture through performance and combat.
    • Exodites: Live on maiden worlds, maintaining a more primitive lifestyle.
    • Ynnari: A new faction centered around the belief in Ynnead, the god of death.

Orks

  • A brutal and warlike species that thrives on conflict.
  • Known for their crude technology, which works through a combination of basic engineering and a latent psychic field.
  • Led by Warbosses, who unite Ork tribes into large warbands known as Waaaghs!

Tyranids

  • A terrifying and insatiable swarm of bio-engineered organisms.
  • Invade and consume entire planets, stripping them of all biomass.
  • Controlled by a hive mind, making them a coordinated and relentless force.
  • Include various bioforms such as Genestealers, Hormagaunts, and monstrous creatures like the Carnifex and Hive Tyrant.

Necrons

  • An ancient race of skeletal robots.
  • Possess advanced technology and the ability to self-repair.
  • Seek to reclaim their lost empire and exterminate all other life.
  • Ruled by the Necron Overlords and their courts.

Tau

  • A technologically advanced and rapidly expanding empire.
  • Promote a philosophy called the "Greater Good," which encourages cooperation and unity among different species.
  • Include various castes such as Fire Warriors (military), Ethereals (leaders), and auxiliaries from other species.

Genestealer Cults

  • Hybrid human-alien cults created by the infiltration of Genestealers (a vanguard organism of the Tyranids).
  • Aim to destabilize and take over human worlds from within, preparing them for eventual consumption by the Tyranid hive fleets.
  • Known for their insidious influence and ability to blend in with human populations.

Dark Eldar (Drukhari)

  • A sadistic and cruel offshoot of the Eldar.
  • Reside in the dark city of Commorragh.
  • Engage in piracy, raiding, and slavery, feeding off the pain and suffering of others to sustain themselves.

Harlequins

  • A mysterious faction of the Eldar dedicated to the Laughing God, Cegorach.
  • Perform deadly dances and acrobatics in battle.
  • Act as messengers and preservers of Eldar culture and mythology.

Kroot

  • A mercenary species that often fights alongside the Tau.
  • Known for their ability to evolve by consuming the genetic material of their enemies.

Vespid

  • Insectoid aliens allied with the Tau.
  • Serve as fast attack troops in the Tau Empire's military forces.

These xenos factions each have their own unique cultures, technologies, and motivations, making the Warhammer 40,000 universe a rich and diverse setting filled with conflict and intrigue.
