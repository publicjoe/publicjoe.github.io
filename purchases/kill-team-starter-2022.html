Kill Team: Starter Set 2022

The Kill Team: Starter Set 2022 is a boxed set from Games Workshop that provides everything needed to start playing Kill Team, a skirmish-level game set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. This set is designed for new players and includes a variety of miniatures, terrain pieces, and rules to help players get started with the game.

The set includes two complete Kill Teams, one for the Death Korps of Krieg and one for the Orks, along with a selection of terrain pieces to create a battlefield. The miniatures are unpainted and require assembly, making it a great option for hobbyists who enjoy building and painting miniatures.

In addition to the miniatures and terrain, the Kill Team: Starter Set 2022 also includes a rulebook that provides players with the necessary rules and guidelines for playing Kill Team. This makes it an excellent choice for beginners who are new to the game, as well as for experienced players looking to expand their collection.

Overall, the Kill Team: Starter Set 2022 is a comprehensive and engaging set that offers a great introduction to the world of Kill Team, providing players with everything they need to start their journey in this exciting tabletop game.
