HeroQuest: Frozen Horror

"HeroQuest: Frozen Horror" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. This expansion was first released in 1991 and introduces a new adventure set in a frozen, icy environment. The storyline of the expansion revolves around the heroes being tasked with investigating a mysterious fortress that has appeared in the northern wastes, which is said to be inhabited by undead creatures and other dangers.

The expansion includes new quest scenarios, additional miniatures, and new terrain pieces that reflect the icy setting. Players take on the roles of the heroes as they embark on quests to uncover the secrets of the Frozen Horror and battle against the undead forces that reside within the fortress.

With its chilling atmosphere and engaging storyline, "HeroQuest: Frozen Horror" is a must-have for fans of the original HeroQuest game. It offers players the opportunity to continue their adventures in the fantasy world of HeroQuest and face new challenges as they explore the frozen landscape and confront the horrors that lurk within.

This expansion is designed to be used in conjunction with the original HeroQuest game, and players will need the core box to fully enjoy the new content and features introduced in "Frozen Horror."

Contents

The "HeroQuest: Frozen Horror" expansion includes the following components:

  • Quest Book: A 10-part quest book featuring the campaign to investigate the Frozen Horror.
  • Accessories: Pad of Character Sheets
  • Miniatures: 23 plastic figures, including:
    • Frozen Horror
    • Barbarian with shield
    • 12 Mercenaries
    • 3 Ice Gremlins
    • 2 Polar Warbears
    • 2 Yetis
    • 2 Dungeon Doors
  • Punchboard: Contains various cardboard tokens and overlays, such as:
    • Bottomless Chasm Room/Live Fog Room
    • Cage Room/Sceptre Room
    • Crystal Key Square
    • Double Slippery Ice
    • Ice Cave Entrance/Seat of Power Room
    • Ice Gremlin Treasure Room/Ice Vault
    • Ice Ledge/Frozen Crypt Room
    • Frosty Altar
    • 6 x Ice Tunnel Square
    • 6 x Magic Ice Square
    • 6 x Skull Square
    • 2 x Skull/Slippery Ice Square
    • 6 x Slippery Ice/Blocked Square
    • Slippery Ice/Ice Vault
    • Triple Slippery Ice/Icy River
  • Cards: 35 Game Cards, including:
    • Barbarian character card
    • Six Treasure cards
    • Four Equipment cards
    • Ten Artifact cards
    • Six Dread Spell cards
    • Eight Monster cards
