HeroQuest: Kellar's Keep

"HeroQuest: Kellar's Keep" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. This expansion was first released in 1991 and adds new adventures and more monsters to the original game. The storyline of the expansion revolves around the evil wizard Kellar, who has taken over a fortress and is threatening the land with his dark magic.

The expansion includes new quest scenarios and additional miniatures. Players take on the roles of the heroes, as they embark on quests to defeat Kellar and his minions.

With its engaging storyline, "HeroQuest: Kellar's Keep" is a must-have for fans of the original HeroQuest game. It offers players the opportunity to continue their adventures in the fantasy world of HeroQuest and face new challenges as they battle against the forces of evil.

This expansion is designed to be used in conjunction with the original HeroQuest game, and players will need the core box to fully enjoy the new content and features introduced in "Kellar's Keep."

Contents

The "HeroQuest: Kellar's Keep" expansion includes the following components:

  • Quest Book: A 10-part quest book featuring the campaign to defeat Kellar.
  • Miniatures: 19 plastic figures, including:
    • 8 Orcs
    • 6 Goblins
    • 3 Abominations
    • 2 Dungeon Doors
  • Punchboard: Contains various cardboard tokens and overlays, such as:
    • Four short stairways
    • Two long stairways
    • Cliff Corridoor
    • Giant Stone Boulder
    • Two trap door tokens
    • Two pit trap tokens
    • Twelve Blocked Square Tiles
    • Weapons Forge
    • Cloud of Dread
    • Statue Tile/li>
    • 4 Part Stone Map
  • Cards: 14 Game Cards, including:
    • Ten Artifact cards
    • Four Equipment cards
