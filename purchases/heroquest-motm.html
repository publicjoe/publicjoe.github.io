HeroQuest: The Mage of the Mirror

"HeroQuest: The Mage of the Mirror" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. This expansion was first released in 1991 and introduces a new quest and additional miniatures to the original game. The storyline of the expansion revolves around a powerful mage who has been trapped in a magical mirror and seeks to escape.

The expansion includes a new quest scenario and additional miniatures. Players take on the roles of the heroes, as they embark on a quest to free the princess.

With its engaging storyline, "HeroQuest: The Mage of the Mirror" is a must-have for fans of the original HeroQuest game. It offers players the opportunity to continue their adventures in the fantasy world of HeroQuest and face new challenges as they battle against the forces of evil.

This expansion is designed to be used in conjunction with the original HeroQuest game, and players will need the core box to fully enjoy the new content and features introduced in "The Mage of the Mirror."

Contents

The "HeroQuest: The Mage of the Mirror" expansion includes the following components:

  • Quest Book: A 10-part quest book featuring the campaign to free the princess.
  • Miniatures: 33 plastic figures, including:
    • 1 Elf Hero
    • 1 Elven Archmage
    • 4 Elven Archers
    • 4 Elven Warriors
    • 3 Giant Wolves
    • 4 Ogres
    • 1 Fireplace
    • 1 Weapons rack
    • 2 Bookcases
    • 1 Throne
    • 3 Chests
    • 1 Alchemist's bench
    • 1 Tomb
    • 1 Wooden Door
    • 1 Iron Door
    • 4 Portcullis'
  • Punchboard: Contains various cardboard tokens and overlays, such as:
    • Four secret door tokens
    • Four pit trap tokens
    • Six blocked/rubble tile counters
  • Cards: 35 Game Cards, including:
    • 1 Hero card (Elf)
    • 4 Monster cards (Ogre, Elf Warrior, Elf Archer, Giant Wolf)
    • 8 Elf Spell cards
    • 4 Treasure cards
    • 10 Artifact cards
    • 4 Equipment cards
    • 7 Dread Spell cards
    • Six blocked/rubble tile counters
