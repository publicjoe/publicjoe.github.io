Imperium Magazine 53 - Triarch Stalker part 1

The Triarch Stalker is a fast attack walker used by the Necrons. It is armed with a pair of heavy gauss cannons mounted on its arms, and can be equipped with a variety of secondary weapons, including a particle caster or a tesla cannon.

The Triarch Stalker is a highly mobile and versatile unit, capable of engaging both infantry and vehicles. It is often used as a skirmisher or flanking unit, taking advantage of its speed and firepower to disrupt enemy formations.

In addition to its combat capabilities, the Triarch Stalker is also equipped with advanced sensors and communication systems, allowing it to coordinate with other Necron units on the battlefield.

Overall, the Triarch Stalker is a formidable and deadly unit that is a key component of the Necron army.
