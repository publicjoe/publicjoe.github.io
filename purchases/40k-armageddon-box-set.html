Armageddon Box Set

The War for Armageddon begins, and Imperial defenders face an Ork Waaagh! of unbelievable proportions. Many noble chapters of Astartes answer the call for aid just in time to face the immeasurable green tide of Ghazghkull Thraka and his trusted bosses. With Armageddon on the balance once again, who will prevail in the 11th edition of Warhammer 40,000?

Trained to the highest standard, Space Marines bring discipline, skill and overwhelming firepower to every battlefield. They act as the Imperium’s elite warriors. Their presence often turns the tide of battle.

Orks are savage, warlike greenskins who thrive on conflict, using numbers, brute force and crude technology to overwhelm their foes. They fight with chaos and unpredictability, making them dangerous in every engagement. Their sheer aggression drives them forward.
