HeroQuest: Return of the Witch Lord

"HeroQuest: Return of the Witch Lord" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. This expansion was first released in 1993 and adds new adventures and more monsters to the original game. The storyline of the expansion revolves around the return of the evil Witch Lord, who seeks to conquer the land once again.

The expansion includes new quest scenarios and additional miniatures. Players take on the roles of the heroes, as they embark on quests to thwart the Witch Lord's plans.

With its engaging storyline, "HeroQuest: Return of the Witch Lord" is a must-have for fans of the original HeroQuest game. It offers players the opportunity to continue their adventures in the fantasy world of HeroQuest and face new challenges as they battle against the forces of evil.

This expansion is designed to be used in conjunction with the original HeroQuest game, and players will need the core box to fully enjoy the new content and features introduced in "Return of the Witch Lord."

Contents

The "HeroQuest: Return of the Witch Lord" expansion includes the following components:

  • Quest Book: A 10-part quest book featuring the campaign to defeat the Witch Lord.
  • Miniatures: 18 plastic figures, including:
    • 8 Skeletons
    • 4 Mummies
    • 4 Zombies
    • 2 Dungeon Doors
  • Punchboard: Contains various cardboard tokens and overlays, such as:
    • Four coffin tiles
    • Four secret door tokens
    • Two pit trap tokens
    • A revolving room tile
    • A death mist tile
    • Six blocked/rubble tile counters
    • A throne room tile
  • Cards: 14 Game Cards, including:
    • Ten Artifact cards
    • Four Equipment cards
