Cadia Stands

Cadia Stands is a boxed set from Games Workshop that includes miniatures, terrain, and rules for playing Warhammer 40,000. It is designed to provide players with everything they need to start playing the game, including a variety of models and terrain pieces that can be used to create a battlefield.

The set typically includes a mix of infantry, vehicles, and terrain pieces, allowing players to create diverse and engaging battles. The models are usually unpainted and require assembly, making it a great option for hobbyists who enjoy building and painting miniatures.

In addition to the miniatures and terrain, Cadia Stands also includes a rulebook that provides players with the necessary rules and guidelines for playing Warhammer 40,000. This makes it an excellent choice for beginners who are new to the game, as well as for experienced players looking to expand their collection.

Overall, Cadia Stands is a comprehensive and engaging set that offers a great introduction to the world of Warhammer 40,000, providing players with everything they need to start their journey in this exciting tabletop game.
