40k Combat Patrol: Orks (2022)

The Warhammer 40,000 Combat Patrol: Orks (2022) is a boxed set designed for players who want to start or expand their Ork army in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. This set provides a variety of Ork miniatures, including infantry and vehicles, allowing players to create a balanced and effective force for tabletop battles.

The set typically includes a warboss, a mix of Ork Boyz, a Deff Dread, and a trio of Deffkoptas. The miniatures are unpainted and require assembly, making it an excellent choice for hobbyists who enjoy building and painting their own models.

Overall, the Warhammer 40,000 Combat Patrol: Orks (2022) is a comprehensive and engaging set that offers a great introduction to the Ork faction in Warhammer 40,000, providing players with everything they need to start their journey in this exciting tabletop game.
