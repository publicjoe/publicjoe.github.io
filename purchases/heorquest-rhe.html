HeroQuest: Rogue Heir of Elethorn

"HeroQuest: Rogue Heir of Elethorn" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. Independent, but loyal to the monarch, The Rogue Heir of Elethorn is a nimble skirmisher deadly with small blades.

Contents

The "HeroQuest: Rogue Heir of Elethorn" expansion includes the following components:

  • Miniatures: 2 plastic figures, including:
    • Rogue Heir of Elethorn - Male
    • Rogue Heir of Elethorn - Female
  • Cards: 17 Game Cards, including:
    • 2 x Rogue Heir of Elethorn Character Cards
    • 6 x Rogue Heir of Elethorn Equipment Cards
    • 9 x Rogue Heir of Elethorn Spell Cards
