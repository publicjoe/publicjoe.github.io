Kill Team: Hivestorm

Kill Team: Hivestorm is the launch set for a new edition of the miniature skirmish game Warhammer 40,000: Kill Team, published by Games Workshop. Set on the Imperial world of Volkus within the Chalnath Expanse, it introduces thrilling skirmish warfare as forces battle over the planet's vital manufacturing hubs and defensive networks.

Volkus Planetary Defense: The centerpiece of the conflict revolves around the "Massif Ballistus," a colossal weapon capable of launching city-block-sized shells over vast distances. Alien invaders descend to disable the Great Gun, while elite Imperial defenders are dispatched to secure it.

Two new kill teams: The box includes 22 new Citadel miniatures, split into two completely fresh factions with various special operatives and customization options.

  • Tempestus Aquilons: Elite Imperial defenders dispatched to secure the Great Gun. Hand-picked Tempestus Scions who specialize in airborne grav-drops, they exhibit high aggression and are skilled in close-quarters combat.
  • Vespid Stingwings: Airborne alien auxiliaries integrated into the T'au Empire. These insectoid raiders strike at the gigantic weapon using their mobility and advanced weaponry to overwhelm defenders.

    • Modular terrain: The set features Killzone: Volkus terrain, providing a variety of Imperial ruins and defensive structures. This terrain encourages dynamic vertical movement and tight firefights representing the streets of Hive Fissilicus.

    Game accessories: The box contains everything needed for play, including a double-sided game board, kill team upgrade tokens, dice, combat gauges, and an Approved Operations card pack.

    Two rulebooks: A copy of the 112-page Kill Team Core Book is included, alongside a separate 72-page Hivestorm Dossier. The Dossier provides lore for Volkus and the Chalnath Expanse, rules for the two new teams, and special campaign missions.
