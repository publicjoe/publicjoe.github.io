Kill Team: Into the Dark

Kill Team: Into the Dark is a boxed set expansion for the miniature skirmish game Warhammer 40,000: Kill Team, published by Games Workshop. Released in 2022, it was the first box set of the game's "second season" and introduced the "Close Quarters" combat rules for fighting inside the narrow confines of a derelict space hulk called the Gallowdark.

Close Quarters rules: Into the Dark changed the gameplay setting from open battlefields to the cramped interiors of a spaceship. The new "Close Quarters" rules focus on brutal, tight-corridor fighting and replace some standard Kill Team mechanics, such as removing vantage points for operatives and introducing interactive doors.

Two new kill teams: The box includes 24 Citadel miniatures, split into two new factions with multiple customization options.

  • Imperial Navy Breachers: An elite Imperial boarding party, equipped with combat shotguns, shields, and other breaching tools.
  • Kroot Farstalker Kinband: A group of Kroot mercenaries seeking plunder within the space hulk.

Modular terrain: The set features 54 modular terrain pieces and barricades, which can be configured in a huge variety of layouts to create different rooms and corridors. The terrain was designed to be compatible with future releases in the Gallowdark series.

Game accessories: The box contains everything needed for play, including a double-sided game board, combat gauges, dice, tokens, and two sets of Tactical Ops cards.

Two rulebooks: A copy of the full Kill Team Core Book is included, along with a separate Into the Dark book. The expansion book provides lore for the Gallowdark, rules for the two new teams, and special campaign and mission packs for narrative and matched play.
