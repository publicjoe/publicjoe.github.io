HeroQuest: Against the Ogre Horde

"HeroQuest: Against the Ogre Horde" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. This expansion adds new adventures and more monsters to the original game. Zargon’s latest plot has ensnared the Dirgrusht horde, a powerful and secretive clan of ogres to the north, that have become increasingly violent. As a formidable Druid hero, you must enter the World’s End Arena to prove your mettle as a warrior, locate the hidden lair of the ogre horde, and weed out the root of corruption at the heart of their clan.

The expansion includes 10 new quests and additional miniatures. Players can take on the role of a druid.

This expansion is designed to be used in conjunction with the original HeroQuest game, and players will need the core box to fully enjoy the new content and features introduced in "Against the Ogre Torment."
