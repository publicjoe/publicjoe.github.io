HeroQuest: Jungles of Delthrak

"HeroQuest: Jungles of Delthrak" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. This expansion adds new adventures and more monsters to the original game. In the dense jungle surrounding the mountains at World’s Edge, an ancient dwarven civilization finds new roots. A sacred artifact prized by the dwarven refugees of Kellar’s Keep has been stolen, and a blight has infected the jungle to its roots.

The expansion includes 16 new quests and additional miniatures. Players can take on the role of a Berserker.

This expansion is designed to be used in conjunction with the original HeroQuest game, and players will need the core box to fully enjoy the new content and features introduced in "Jungles of Delthrak."
