HeroQuest: Path of the Wandering Monk

"HeroQuest: Path of the Wandering Monk" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest. The graceful Monk hero is a student of unarmed combat techniques formed in reverence to the 4 elements.

Contents

The "HeroQuest: Path of the Wandering Monk" expansion includes the following components:

  • Miniatures: 2 plastic figures, including:
    • Monk - Male
    • Monk - Female
  • Cards: 7 Game Cards, including:
    • 2 x Monk Character Cards
    • 4 x Elementary Style Cards
    • 1 x Elementary Explanation Card
