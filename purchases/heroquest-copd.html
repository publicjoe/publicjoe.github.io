HeroQuest: Crypt of Perpetual Darkness

"HeroQuest: Crypt of Perpetual Darkness" is an expansion for the classic board game HeroQuest.

Zargon’s minions are at your doorstep, threatening the place you call home. Armed only with your effects and an ancient map of elvish origin, you must journey deep underground in search of a long-lost crypt rumoured to contain a powerful artifact. Keep this treasure from Zargon – you’re the only hope of your people!
