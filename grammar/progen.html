Pronouns of the Genitive Case

The primary use of the Russian genitive case is to show possession, where the possessor always follows the object it possess.

Here are the Russian pronouns that can be used as the object of a sentence.

Меня́ (myen-ya)

Me

Тебя́ (teb-ya) informal

You

Его́ (ye-vo)

Him

Её (ye-yo)

Her

Нас (nas)

Us

Вас (vas) formal, or plural

You

Их (ikh) formal, or plural

Them
