My Paint Scheme

Prime with Wraithbone spray can

Battle Ready

Paints in order

  1. Paint 2 coats out Contrast Space Wolves Grey
  2. Drybruh all over with Russ Grey
  3. Drybruh all over with Fenrisian Grey
  4. Paint the ribbing with Contrast Black Templar
  5. Paint the skin with Contrast Guilliman Flesh
  6. Paint the gun with Contrast Basilicanum Grey, along with the pistol handle
  7. Paint the gun casing with Contrast Black Templar
  8. Paint the pistol holster and any pouches/straps with Contrast Snakebite Leather
  9. Paint the chest eagle with Retributor Armour
  10. Wash the chest eagle with Contrast Guilliman Flesh
  11. Highlight with Auric Armour Gold
  12. Paint the left shoulder pad with Averland Sunset
  13. Paint the left shoulder pad with Yriel Yellow leaving a small amount of the Averland Sunset showing round the edge. (This may take 2 or 3 coats)
  14. Paint the eye lenses of the helmet with wraithbone, then Contrast Blood Angels Red

For Intercessors:

  1. Paint the right shoulder pad with Mephiston Red
  2. Paint the right shoulder pad with Evil Sunz Scarlet leaving a small amount of the Mephiston Red showing round the edge
  3. Paint the pack Markings with Abaddon Black
