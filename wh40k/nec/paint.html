Battle Ready

Paints in order

  1. Paint the body amour of the necron with Runelord Brass
  2. Wash the armour with Contrast Snakebite Leather
  3. Drybrush the armour with Canoptek Alloy
  4. Paint the gun and joints with 2 coats of Contrast Black Templar
  5. Highlight with Dawnstone
  6. Paint the weapon circles with Ceramite White
  7. Wash these with Tesseract Glow
